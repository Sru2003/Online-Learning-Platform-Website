3 Skills For Kids To Succeed In Life!

1. Communication Skills One of the most crucial life skills for kids is effective communication. This skill will be useful in every area of their lives, from personal relationships to future careers. Help your children develop strong communication skills by encouraging them to express themselves clearly and concisely. Also, teach active listening skills to kids so they can understand others.
2. Problem-Solving Skills for kids Another essential life skill that your children need to develop is problem-solving. This skill will come in handy when they encounter challenges in their lives. Teach your children how to identify the root cause of a problem and brainstorm possible solutions. Also, encourage them to think critically and creatively to come up with the best solution.
3. Decision-Making Skills for kids Making decisions is another essential life skill that your children need to develop. This skill will help them to make the right choices in life. Teach your children how to weigh the pros and cons of a situation before deciding. Also, encourage them to listen to their intuition and follow their gut instinct.

Why Teaching Kids Life Skills Is Just As Important As Academics!

1. Self-care
One of the most important life skills for kids is self-care. This includes essential things like hygiene, nutrition, and exercise. Teaching kids how to take care of their bodies will help them stay healthy and prevent illnesses.
2. Money management
Another important life skill for kids is money management. This means learning how to save, spend, and donate money wisely. Teaching kids about money will help them make responsible decisions with their finances as they grow up.
3. Time management Time management is another important life skill for kids to learn. This includes setting goals, using a planner and managing tasks efficiently. Teaching kids how to manage their time will help them stay organised and accomplish more in life.
4. Problem-solving Problem-solving is another key life skill for kids to learn. This includes identifying problems, brainstorming solutions and using critical thinking to find the best solution. Teaching kids how to solve problems will help them handle challenges in life and make better decisions.

All About Rabindranath Tagore

Rabindra Jayanthi is coming up on May 9th, and it’s a great opportunity to learn more about one of India’s most famous poets! Rabindranath Tagore was born in 1861 and wrote his famous Tagore poetry, songs, and stories that celebrated nature, simplicity, and spiritual peace. He was also an accomplished artist, musician, and architect.Rabindranath began writing his so-called Tagore poetry as a young boy and by the age of 16, he had composed several poems in Bengali. In 1877, his first collection of poems, Kantar Moru, was published. He also wrote novels, essays, and plays. His most famous work is Gitanjali (Song Offerings), a collection of 103 poems that was published in 1910.
